Director's Message

Welcome to My Own Science School.

Since our establishment in 2012, our vision has been to bring meaningful and quality education to our community.

We started this journey with limited resources but with a strong commitment to making a positive difference in the lives of our students. Over the years, we have focused not only on academic excellence but also on developing confident, responsible, and morally upright individuals.

By integrating practical learning, modern teaching methodologies, and educational technology, we strive to prepare our students for the challenges of the 21st century while nurturing strong Islamic values and ethical character.

Today, after more than thirteen years of dedicated service, we take pride in the achievements of our alumni who are serving as doctors, engineers, teachers, entrepreneurs, and professionals in various fields. The greatest recognition of our efforts is the trust of our community.

At My Own Science School, we believe that education is not merely about academic success; it is about shaping character, inspiring lifelong learning, and preparing young minds to become compassionate, capable, and responsible citizens.

We sincerely thank our parents for their continued trust and partnership, and we remain committed to providing a safe, innovative, and nurturing learning environment where every child can discover their potential and build a brighter future.

With sincere regards, Aftab Khan Director, My Own Science School
